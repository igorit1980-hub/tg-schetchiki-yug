# Channel Content Templates

## Project context

Project:
- Telegram storefront / Mini App for `Счетчики Юг`

Channel style:
- similar to BigSaleApple in mechanics
- adapted for engineering equipment
- adapted for B2B and wholesale sales

Core task of the channel:
- show products
- show promotions
- show selections
- route clients to manager
- later route clients to bot and Mini App

## Recommended first product set

For initial channel filling:
- 5 water meters
- 3 to 5 gas meters
- 3 to 5 pumps
- 3 to 5 accessories

## Content rules

When adapting templates:
- keep posts short
- do not overload with technical text
- show price clearly
- show stock or order status
- always end with a call to action
- use one clean photo per post at minimum
